About Us

Air Canada Rouge Virtual is a vibrant and dedicated community for flight simulation enthusiasts who share a passion for the Air Canada Rouge experience. We strive for realism, professionalism, and most importantly, fun! Our virtual airline operates with the same routes, fleet, and operational standards as the real Air Canada Rouge, providing an immersive and challenging environment for our pilots.

Whether you're an experienced virtual aviator or just starting your journey, you'll find a welcoming community, comprehensive resources, and exciting opportunities to explore the world from the cockpit of an Air Canada Rouge aircraft. We believe in fostering a supportive environment where pilots can learn, grow, and share their love for aviation.

  • Realistic Operations: Fly real-world routes and schedules.
  • Dedicated Community: Connect with fellow aviation enthusiasts.
  • Continuous Learning: Access resources to enhance your flying skills.
  • Global Network: Explore destinations across the globe.

Our Operations

At Air Canada Rouge Virtual, we pride ourselves on simulating real-world airline operations as closely as possible. Our system tracks your flights, hours, and achievements, providing a rewarding experience for every pilot.

We offer a dynamic route network covering all destinations served by the real Air Canada Rouge, from vibrant city centers to exotic leisure getaways. Pilots can choose from a variety of flight types, including scheduled passenger services, charter flights, and special events.
